Welcome to Your Health Center

Silo Hospital is a 350-bedded multi-super specialty, modern, patient–friendly “Green Hospital “providing a range of outpatient and inpatient preventive, diagnostic and treatment services. SILOHEALTH Hospital provides world-class treatment and excellent healthcare services in India, offering best treatment for most diseases and medical problems with one of the highest success rates in India.Delivering the highest standards of global healthcare, SILOHEALTH Hospital is accredited by JCI – Joint Commission International (USA), NABH (National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers) and NABL (National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories) for providing quality healthcare and patient safety across India. Spread across three wings, SILOHEALTH West, SILOHEALTH East and SILOHEALTH North, SILOHEALTH Hospital has a spacious physical infrastructure providing a ‘green ‘healing ambience for patients.

SILOHEALTH Hospital super specialist Medical Team is one of the best in the country with a high level of experience and unmatched expertise. Their commitment to compassionate patient care is backed by a dedicated nursing and para medical team to deliver quality medical care and treatment to its patients. SILOHEALTH Hospital has always stressed on innovation to provide life-saving care to its patients. Equipped with world-class technology, SILOHEALTH Hospital has successfully performed pioneering treatments establishing newer standards for care in the state and country. SILOHEALTH Hospital has evolved into an epic hospital in Ahmedabad with a culture to deliver human and compassionate care to its patients.
